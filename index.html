FAQ

How do I sign up?
You can either sign up via our mobile app, or on our website, whichever is easier.
What problem are you trying to solve?
We are trying to address a huge problem that we all face everyday. We understand how difficult it is to safely store every piece of receipt and enter it manually just before tax-time, or the end of the month. Most of the employees need to submit their bills to finance for clearance, for which they have to spend time and effort manually typing in all this data into a spreadsheet to submit. We feel that this time can go towards something more productive for the company. What we are trying to achieve, is a hassle free, digitization of not-only receipts but every piece of important data you need secured.
What phones & platforms are currently supported?
Works on all models of Android phone as of now. We will come to iOS, BB10, Ubuntu, Firefox OS & Windows shortly.
