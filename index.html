Piggy Bank is a child-friendly, cartoon pig-based, interface that is used to encourage responsible saving and monetary habits from an early age. The interface uses a cartoon piggy-bank to allow a child to see their money inside of a real bank account rather than dry numbers and statements. It also provides a handy tool to allow grandparents and relatives to make deposits accompanied by messages and avatars for the child to read.

Saving for a specific purpose is encouraged by the use of "piglets." A child can assign a piglet that will appear next to the main piggy-bank and move money from the main account and into the sub-account. As the account grows towards its set objective, the piglet will expand in size.
