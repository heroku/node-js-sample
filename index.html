Physiotherapy sessions usually start in a hospital environment with full blown medical support and regularity. However, as time goes by and costs go up, subjects undergoing physiotherapy sessions go home with few advice, and just the conscious obligation to checkup with their therapist from time to time. This leads to a lack of medical control over how the home exercises are executed, usually delaying the rehabilitation period.

By incorporating Multibody Dynamics algorithms in parallel with a hip game-like interface, Physio@Home provides the customer with an appealing solution for practicing their physiotherapy routines at home. In addition, the whole system will be able to store detailed information in a local secure database. This data can be shared formated in a detailed report either directly via the internet to the Health Professional following the subject, or in person when the subject goes to their medical appointment.
